About us

Welcome to IPSO, the Indian Patient Safety Outreach Organization, dedicated to advancing patient safety standards across healthcare facilities in India.

At IPSO, we recognize the paramount importance of patient safety in the healthcare sector. Our mission is to foster a culture of safety, quality, and excellence in healthcare delivery through education, collaboration, and advocacy.

For over a decade, IPSO - the Indian Patient Safety Outreach Organization, has been at the forefront of promoting patient safety excellence through strategic collaborations with esteemed healthcare institutions across India. Among our notable collaborations are partnerships with premier hospitals such as AIIMS, Apollo, MAX, Medanta and many more.

These collaborations represent a shared commitment to advancing patient safety, quality improvement, and excellence in healthcare delivery. By harnessing the collective expertise and resources of our partner institutions, IPSO aims to drive positive change and make a meaningful impact on patient safety outcomes nationwide.



Our Vision

  • Our vision is to create a healthcare ecosystem in India where patient safety is prioritized at every level of care delivery. We envision a future where preventable medical errors are minimized, and every patient receives safe, effective, and compassionate care.

Who We Are

  • IPSO is a non-profit organization comprised of healthcare professionals, patient advocates, policymakers, and industry experts passionate about improving patient safety outcomes. Our diverse team brings together expertise from various disciplines to address the multifaceted challenges in patient safety.

Why choose IPSO

  • Expertise: Benefit from the expertise of seasoned professionals in patient safety, healthcare quality, and risk management.
  • Collaboration: Join a collaborative network of healthcare stakeholders committed to driving positive change in patient safety.
  • Impact: Make a tangible impact on patient safety outcomes and contribute to the advancement of healthcare quality in India.
  • Innovation: Stay updated on the latest innovations and best practices in patient safety through our educational programs and research initiatives.
